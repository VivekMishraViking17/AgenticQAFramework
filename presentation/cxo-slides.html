Agentic QE:
AI Speed. Human Quality.

The Problem

  • 4–8 hours manual test design per Jira story
  • Security, edge, and UAT coverage inconsistently applied
  • Automation backlog growing faster than SDET capacity
  • Weak traceability from story → test → defect

The Answer

  • Cursor AI agents read Jira, Confluence, PRDs, bug history
  • Auto-generate labeled test cases in under 15 minutes
  • QA SME mandatory review before XRay publish
  • Automation agents generate Cypress/Playwright scripts

Measurable Impact

8-phase agentic lifecycle · 11 agents · 6 human gates · Illustrative model: 4 squads (~16 QE FTE), 200 stories/year
